Whipped Cream recipe

Description

A simple step-by-step guide to make basic Whipped cream with just three ingredient.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon confectioners' sugar

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, whip cream until stiff peaks are just about to form.
  2. Then beat in vanilla and sugar until peaks form. Make sure not to over-beat, cream will then become lumpy and butter-like.

Nutrition Facts

Per Serving: 54 calories; protein 0.3g; carbohydrates 0.9g; fat 5.5g; cholesterol 20.4mg; sodium 5.7mg.
